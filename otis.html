Oti's Journal: Prince in pieces

A charming, story-driven 3D adventure where love leads the way. Embark on an emotional journey across a beautiful archipelago to find the scattered pieces of your beloved husband.

Key Mechanics

  • Island Hopping with Oti: Travel from island to island with your loyal companion, Oti the Whale. Explore diverse, hand-crafted 3D environments to uncover secrets and progress through a heartwarming narrative.
  • Treasure Hunting: Search each island thoroughly to locate hidden treasure chests. Inside these chests lie the crucial fragments of maps that lead to your next destination.
  • Map Restoration Puzzles: Progress isn't just handed to you. When you find torn map pieces, you must engage in a jigsaw-style puzzle mini-game. Move, rotate, and combine the torn fragments perfectly to reveal the complete map.
  • Clue Following: Once a map is successfully restored, carefully follow its traces and environmental clues to unlock the path to the next island and edge closer to putting your prince back together.

Controls

  • Movement: W, A, S, D (Explore the 3D environments).
  • Camera / Interaction: Mouse (Look around and interact with chests/objects).
  • Map Puzzles: Left Mouse Button (Click and drag to move and combine map pieces).
