MirrorBlade

A fast-paced action-roguelike where you defend a central core using your blade and synchronized reflections to slice enemies and parry projectiles.

MirrorBlade was developed for the Aydın Game Jam, where it was awarded 1st Place. The game challenges players to protect the heart of the universe against relentless waves of enemies. By controlling a character and its synchronized mirrors, players must utilize precise movement, tactical positioning, and timing to survive the onslaught.

Developers

  • Efe Görkem Akkanat
  • Görkem Altıntaş

Key Mechanics

  • Parry and Reflect: Use your attack to deflect enemy projectiles. Reflected projectiles can be redirected toward enemies to deal massive damage.
  • Dynamic Movement: Navigate the circular arena while maintaining a strategic position relative to the Core.
  • Dash and Slash: Use the dash ability to reposition quickly or pierce through enemy lines.
  • Modular Architecture: Built using an event-driven, decoupled architecture for high performance and easy extensibility.

Controls

  • Rotate: Move Mouse (Character follows the cursor direction).
  • Move: Click and Hold Left Mouse Button (Move towards the cursor).
  • Attack: Space Bar (Melee strike and projectile parry).
  • Dash: Left Shift (Quick burst of movement, consumes mana).
