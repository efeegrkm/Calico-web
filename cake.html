Not a piece of cake

An innovative, fast-paced tactical board game set on a dynamic circular grid. Awarded 2nd Place and Best Sound Design at the Rakun Game Jam, this game challenges you to outsmart your opponent in a race to find the hidden treasure.

Key Mechanics

  • Dynamic Circular Movement: Navigate through floating stones arranged in concentric circles. Plan your path carefully, as every square can harbor a hidden trap such as a bomb, a piston, or a teleporter.
  • Tactical Card System: Upon successfully landing on a square, play one of the 6 tactical cards from your hand. Use these cards to teleport your opponent, swap positions, cancel their previous moves, or reverse the board's rotation.
  • Ring Manipulation: Instead of playing a card, you have the option to physically rotate the circular rings of the board. Shift the terrain to open new paths for yourself or block your opponent's progress.
  • The Race for Treasure: Be fast and cunning. The ultimate goal is to find the hidden treasure among the stones while using your traps, cards, and ring manipulation to keep your opponent as far away from the prize as possible.

Controls

  • Navigate: W, A, S, D (Move selection cursor across stones/rings).
  • Confirm Move/Action: Space Bar.
  • Card Selection: Mouse Click (Select and play tactical cards from your hand).
