Not a piece of cake

An innovative, fast-paced tactical board game set on a dynamic circular grid. Awarded 2nd Place and Best Sound Design at the Rakun Game Jam.

Key Mechanics

  • Dynamic Circular Movement: Navigate through floating stones arranged in concentric circles.
  • Tactical Card System: Play one of the 6 tactical cards from your hand to teleport, swap, or cancel moves.
  • Ring Manipulation: Physically rotate the circular rings of the board. Shift the terrain to open new paths.

Controls

  • Navigate: W, A, S, D (Move selection cursor across stones/rings).
  • Confirm: Space Bar.
  • Card Selection: Mouse Click.
