M'Toys

A nostalgic journey that takes us back to our childhood, where we battle with the toys we used to play with. Starting with a child chatting with their grandfather and playing games, you build your own custom character using building blocks and fight against rival toys.

Key Mechanics

  • Character Creation: Build your toy character using a drag-and-drop block mechanic. Stack the blocks, and the game will determine its final shape. Select the 'hand' icon in the bottom left to proceed to the combat phase.
  • Combat System: Engage in battles with your custom-built block character by simply left-clicking on your skills.
  • Dynamic Parry Mechanic: The parry skill functions differently across three levels:
  • Level 1: Hold the Spacebar when the first prompt appears. Release it and press again exactly when the second prompt shows.
  • Level 2: The prompt will appear 3 consecutive times; press the Spacebar precisely at each prompt.
  • Level 3: A single, precise Spacebar press is required when the prompt appears.

Controls

  • Build Character: Left Mouse Button (Drag and Drop blocks).
  • Use Skills: Left Click (Select skills during combat).
  • Parry: Space Bar (Timing varies based on the mechanic level).
